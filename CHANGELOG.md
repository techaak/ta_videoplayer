## 0.0.1

### Initial Release 🚀

Features:
- Android & iOS video player support
- Network video playback
- Asset video playback
- YouTube video support using IDs and URLs
- Playlist support for multiple videos
- Auto preload next video for smooth playback
- Customizable player controls
- Reels/shorts style video mode
- Fullscreen and embedded player support
- Volume and mute/unmute controls
- Seekbar position customization
- Auto play next video support
- Optimized initialization and buffering handling
- Improved scrolling performance for feeds/reels
- Example app included
